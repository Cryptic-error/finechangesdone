Hear from our managing director

Fine Surgicals Nepal Private Limited, is a dynamic and a leading medical equipment and surgical item supplier company based in Kathmandu, Nepal. The company was established in 2017 and has its registered address at Chabahil, Ganesthan, Kathmandu, The company deals with supply of medical equipments, medical machineries, surgical items and medical devices. The company has a wide range of satisfied client portfolio including but not limited to Hospitals, Government Authorities, Local Bodies, Private Organizations, Non-Profit Organization (NPO), International Non-Governmental Organizations (INGO's) besides individual customer. The company is managed by strong and experienced team of professionals including Doctors, Bio-Medical Engineers, Chartered Accountants and industry experts having experience of more than 20 years of experience in medical items and equipment
